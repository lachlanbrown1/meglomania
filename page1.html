OUR AMAZING MENUS

Megalomania's menu is sublime, contemporary cuisine consisting of tapas-style short food, entrees, mains and desserts. Partnered with an extensive range of craft beers, wines and expertly crafted cocktails. Megalomania is the place to go for craft beer in Yeppoon - with a wide selection of rare beer unique to the area and in some cases unique to Queensland, featuring a rotating beer of the month and beer tasting paddles. The wine, spirit and cocktail list are second to none.
